About UPH- Dr. Jose. G Tamayo Medical University

As leaders of medical education, our college provides transformative learning experiences which equip students to excel in today's ever-evolving healthcare arena. Over time, Tamayo Medical University has established itself with academic excellence, cutting edge research initiatives and compassionate patient care services - hallmarks that distinguish any successful institution.

Tamayo Medical University in Laguna, Manila has grown into one of the best medical university Laguna, Manila in Philippines and attract students from other countries. We have always been committed to providing students with low cost medical education and training in modern medical facilities and also partnerships with renowned hospitals and medical institutions in the Philippines, enabling our students to engage in clinical rotations and internships These placements allow students to work alongside experienced medical professionals, gaining valuable insights into the medical field and enhancing their practical skills.

At Tamayo , students can explore anatomy, cadaver, Simulation lab, Medical library, Research Centers physiology, equipped with cutting-edge facilities that facilitate hands-on learning experiences. Our curriculum is designed to provide ample opportunities for students to apply their theoretical knowledge in real-world settings. With experienced faculty available to aid their efforts through engaging teaching methodologies that support complex concepts for greater grasping by the student body.

Moreover, the university offers various student support services, such as academic counselling and career guidance, to ensure the overall well-being and success of MBBS students. Tamayo Medical University also fosters a conducive learning environment through extracurricular activities and student organizations that allow students to engage in social and professional development
