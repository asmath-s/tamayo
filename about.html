About Us

UPH- Dr. Jose G. Tamayo, medical University established in 1976 in Binan, Laguna city is the very first & main campus specializing in medical & health related courses established by The University of Perpetual Help System Laguna, having committed itself to service in the forefront of education and health care, came into being out of the unselfish effort and untiring commitment of its founder: Dr. Jose G. Tamayo and Dr. Josefina Laperal Tamayo.

The desire to serve others was manifested at a very young age when Dr. Jose G. Tamayo, then a young boy dreamt of being a doctor. For him, it was the best way he that he could serve his fellowmen. But when that dream became a reality, he realized that his best was not good enough the services he rendered were so limited and only within the realm of his profession as a doctor. With an ardent desire to serve his fellowmen, the idea of reaching out to through the setting up of an educational institution.

UPH-Dr Jose. G Tamayo Medical University in Manila, Laguna earned a reputation for excellence in medical education and gradually increased our MBBS admission in Philippines, India, Nepal, Thailand, Africa, Bhutan & UAE and other countries. The university has produced many successful medical professionals who have made significant contributions to the field of medicine

Additionally, we encourage our students to actively participate in medical outreach programs and community service initiatives. These experiences not only contribute to their professional growth but also provide a meaningful way to make a positive impact on society.

As an international student at Tamayo Medical University, you will have access to a wide range of practical experiences and internships that will enhance your learning and prepare you for a successful medical career. We are dedicated to providing a supportive environment where you can develop your skills and gain the practical experience essential for your future as a medical professional

If you looking for the best college to shape your medical career, join our vibrant academic community where you will acquire knowledge, skills and compassion necessary for becoming an exceptional healer and providing hope in society
