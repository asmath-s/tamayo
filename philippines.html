About Philippines

The Philippines, a Southeast Asian country renowned for its natural beauty and warm hospitality, also boasts a reputable medical education system. With over 7,000 islands, the country offers a diverse range of landscapes to explore. However, it is also recognized for its high-quality medical education and excellent opportunities for aspiring healthcare professionals

The Philippines is the hub of medical education of Southeast ASIA and it is home to several world-class medical institutions and healthcare facilities. The country has been attracting medical students and healthcare professionals from around the world due to its quality and affordable medical education.

The Philippines have a decent interface with the western world, which makes it the best choice for students from all over the world. Students going to study Medicine will feel at home, as the Filipinos are friendly people. They are incredibly cordial and polite. The Philippines is a safe country and ensures secured living for foreigners who come to study MBBS. Among them, MBBS in the Philippines is being the first choice by Indian student as it caters the course at very affordable rate along with many advantages in contrast to other countries

MBBS Admission Process in Philippines, Abroad

The admission process for medical programs in the Philippines is relatively straightforward. Global students seeking to study MBBS in the Philippines, can take advantage of direct admission, avoiding complex entrance examinations and stringent eligibility criteria. By submitting academic records and other required documents, students can secure their seats in their preferred medical universities.

Educational Qualifications

To be eligible for an MBBS course in the Philippines, Abroad candidates must meet the following educational qualifications

High School Diploma

Applicants should possess a valid high school diploma or an equivalent certification from a recognized educational board.

Science Stream

It is generally preferred for applicants to have completed their high school education in the science stream, with a focus on subjects like Biology, Chemistry, and Physics. These foundational sciences will lay the groundwork for the medical curriculum

Minimum GPA

MBBS programs in the Philippines require applicants to have a minimum Grade Point Average (GPA) of 2.5 or above on a 4.0 scale. However, it's important to note that specific institutions may have varying GPA requirements.

Entrance Examinations

At UPH-Dr. Jose G. Tamayo Medical University, No entrance test for Indian and International Students.

Language Proficiency

Studying MBBS in the Philippines offers the advantage of English as the primary medium of instruction, ensuring language proficiency and ease of communication for international students. This fosters a conducive learning environment and enables seamless interaction with faculty, peers, and patients, enhancing the overall educational experience.

MBBS In Philippines For Indian Students & International Students

The Philippines has emerged as a popular destination for Indian & International students seeking to study MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery) degrees. With its affordable tuition fees, world-class medical education, and English as the medium of instruction, the Philippines offers a favorable environment for Indian students to study medicine.

Studying MBBS in the Philippines has several advantages. The country offers quality education that is internationally recognized. The medium of instruction is English, making it convenient for Indian, & International students. Tuition fees are affordable, and the admission process is straightforward. The cultural compatibility and welcoming environment further enhance the experience. Overall, the Philippines is an excellent choice for Indian students pursuing a career in medicine.

High FMGE / MCI Screening Test Passing Percentage

  • For more than 7 years, students graduating from Philippines have been having the highest FMGE pass percentage.
  • High quality of imparting of education in most of the Philippines Universities and the foreigner friendly Filipinos make it very comfortable for students to enjoy

No language Problem

  • 94% population people in the Philippines speak English and it is the 3rd largest English-speaking country in the world.
  • The biggest advantage of studying MBBS in Philippines as students can communicate with patients fluently unlike in non-English speaking country for better clinical exposure.

R&D in Medicine

  • Doctor of Medicine (M.D) program in Philippines University provides huge opportunity for training and publishing paper & journals in medical research & development for students, which gives a bright scope in their carrier.

Good Clinical Training

  • Medical education in Philippines is considered to provide excellent clinical exposure. Students will have mandatory clerkship training in both government & private hospitals to have excellent clinical skills at all categories of practice

Gateway to PG in USA & International Job opportunities

  • Philippines are the largest supplier of trained nurses & doctors to USA & several other countries. Strong Culture of giving USMLE for Post-graduate residency & practice.
  • Philippine universities are listed in ECFMG & California Board of Medicine for licensing.
  • One of the key advantages of pursuing an MBBS degree in the Philippines is its recognition by the United States Medical Licensing Examination (USMLE). The USMLE is a series of exams that international medical graduates (IMGs) must pass to practice medicine in the USA. Many medical schools in the Philippines are listed in the International Medical Education Directory (IMED), allowing their graduates to be eligible to take the USMLE exams, making it an essential step for those aspiring to practice in the US.
  • Medical Curriculum in Philippines are similar to US Curriculum, enabling seamless integration for aspiring doctors who wish to pursue PG in USA.
